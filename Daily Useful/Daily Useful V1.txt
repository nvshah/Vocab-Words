Improvology
-----------

* Enrage : make angry
* Bout : (mukebaji pratiyogita), contest, battle
* Caprice : sudden change in mood
* Emanate : (nikalna), Emit, 

* Staunch : atal, kattar (very commited in attitude)
* Valiant : Showing Courage
* Reaasert : (Pusti Karna)

* Remonstrate : virodh karna
* Demonstrate : pradarsan karna
* Obligation : duty or comitment
* Exaggerate : bdha chda kar kehna

* Impasse : A situation where no advancement is possible (gatirodh, kathin sthiti)

* Benediction : Prayer for Blessings
* Abase :- Belittle someone, (apmanit karna) (nicha dikhana), defile
* Contigent :- Accidental, unexpected
* Erudite : Knowledgable
* Heedless : Careless Nature
* Repeal : abolish, nullify, Rescind, cancellation, invalidate
* Arbitration : Compromise, concilation, negotiation, (Madhyastha, Panch Faisla)
